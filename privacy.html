Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data NotBuying collects, how it's used, and your rights. Short version: we collect only what we need, we don't sell it, and you can delete it any time.

What we collect

  • Email address — when you join the waitlist or create an account.
  • Content you submit — videos, YouTube links, or post text you paste for analysis. Videos are sent directly to the AI model for analysis and are never stored on our servers.
  • Analysis results — your scores and breakdowns, stored to power your history page (paid users only).
  • Basic usage data — pages visited, actions taken, so we can improve the product.

What we don't collect

  • We do not store your videos. Ever. They are sent to Gemini AI, analysed, and immediately discarded.
  • We do not sell your data to anyone.
  • We do not run ads or let advertisers target you.

How we use your data

  • To send you the one launch email you signed up for (and nothing else unless you opt in).
  • To deliver your analysis results.
  • To improve the product based on how people use it.
  • To process payments securely via Stripe.

Third-party services

We use the following services to run NotBuying:

  • Supabase — database and authentication.
  • Google Gemini AI — content analysis. Content is processed under Google's API terms.
  • Stripe — payments. We never see or store your card details.
  • Beehiiv — email list. You can unsubscribe any time.
  • Vercel — hosting.

Your rights

  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.
  • You can unsubscribe from emails at any time — every email has a one-click unsubscribe link.
  • You can ask what data we hold about you.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at: privacy@NotBuying.site

Cookies

We use minimal cookies — only what's needed for authentication and basic analytics. No tracking cookies, no ad pixels.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes, we'll notify waitlist members by email before the change takes effect.

Questions? Email privacy@NotBuying.site — a real person reads every message.
